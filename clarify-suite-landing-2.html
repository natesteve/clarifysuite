How it works
Three clicks to clarity.

No app switching, no copy-pasting. The explanation comes to you, right on the page you're already reading.

01 — INSTALL

Download free from the store

Install any Clarify extension free from the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons. Register at clarifysuite.app to get your license key.

02 — SET YOUR LEVEL

Tell us your program

Select your program once — RN, Paramedic, DVM, Dental Hygiene, and more — and every explanation is framed for your scope of practice.

03 — HIGHLIGHT

Select any text

Highlight a sentence or paragraph from any textbook, PubMed article, white paper, or clinical guideline on any webpage.

04 — READ

Panel slides in instantly

Right-click → choose your extension → a clean sidebar delivers a plain-English breakdown with clinical context, key takeaway, and pitfalls to watch for.
